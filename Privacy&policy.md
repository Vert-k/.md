# Privacy Policy — Nasiib Bot

**Last updated:** May 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data Nasiib Bot ("the Bot", "we", "us") collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data. By using the Bot in any Discord server, you agree to this policy.

---

## 1. Who We Are

Nasiib Bot is a Discord bot providing economy, gaming, and community features across Discord servers. It is independently developed and operated.

---

## 2. What Data We Collect

When you interact with the Bot, we may store the following information:

### Account & Identity
- Your Discord user ID
- Your Discord username (display name at time of interaction)

### Economy & Progress
- Coin and diamond balances
- XP, level, and prestige
- Daily streak and last claim timestamp
- Vote streak and last vote timestamp
- Owned titles and active title
- Legendary crate count
- XP boost status

### Game Statistics
- Wins, losses, games played, and current/highest streak (overall)
- Per-game stats for: FTI, Bluff, IQ, Guess, Tower, Miino, and Sheeko
- Achievements earned

### Bank System
- Bank name, level, and creation date
- Deposits, profits, and transaction history within the bank system
- Bank investment amounts and claim history
- Guard Dog status (active/inactive)

### Moderation Records
- Warning count
- Mute expiry timestamp (if muted)
- Ban status
- Account hold status and reason (if applicable)

### Server Configuration (Guild-level data)
- Guild (server) ID
- Configured channel IDs (welcome, level-up, starboard, counting)
- Starboard threshold setting
- Reaction roles configuration
- Automod settings

---

## 3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect solely to:

- Run economy commands (coins, diamonds, daily, vote rewards)
- Track game progress, levels, and achievements
- Operate the bank and investment system
- Apply moderation actions set by server administrators
- Send vote reminders and reward users for voting
- Display leaderboards and profile information

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

---

## 4. Data Storage & Security

- All data is stored in a secured PostgreSQL database
- Data is only accessible to the bot and its developer
- We apply industry standard precautions to protect stored data
- We do **not** store Discord passwords, email addresses, or payment information

---

## 5. Third-Party Services

The Bot integrates with the following external service:

- **top.gg** — used for the voting system (`!vote`). When you vote, top.gg notifies the Bot with your Discord user ID. Please review [top.gg's Privacy Policy](https://top.gg/privacy) for how they handle your data.
- **Discord** — all interactions occur within Discord's platform. Please review [Discord's Privacy Policy](https://discord.com/privacy).

---

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as you are an active user of the Bot. If you have not interacted with the Bot for an extended period, your data may remain stored but becomes inactive.

If you wish to have your data deleted, please contact us (see Section 9).

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

The Bot is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13, consistent with Discord's Terms of Service. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child's data has been collected, please contact us immediately.

---

## 8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** — request a summary of the data we hold about you
- **Correction** — request correction of inaccurate data
- **Deletion** — request deletion of your personal data
- **Objection** — object to certain uses of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via the method listed in Section 9.

---

## 9. Contact

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact the developer through the official Nasiib Bot Discord support server or via Discord direct message.

---

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be announced in the official Nasiib Bot server. Continued use of the Bot after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
